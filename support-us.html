Education belongs to everyone. Let's make it a reality.

In Noida, India, many migrant families struggle for survival, living in slums with limited resources. Their children, often overlooked, face a bleak future without education. SOUL Satya Hari School, run by the NGO SOUL, is their beacon of hope.

Why We Educate:

  • Empowering Children: Education equips children to face challenges, dream bigger, and break free from the cycle of poverty.
  • Social Upliftment:Educated children become contributing members of society, uplifting their families and communities.
  • Gender Equality: We prioritize girlsâ€™ education, challenging societal norms and creating a more equitable future.

Our Impacts:

  • From Humble Beginnings:We started with a few children, offering basic learning and life skills.
  • Growing Impact: Today, 60 children receive quality education, including essential subjects and empowering activities like sewing and cooking.
  • Bridge to Opportunity: We help graduating students transition to formal schools through the EWS scheme. (Success stories: 3 in 2015, 4 in 2016, 7 in 2017, 8 in 2018)
  • Benchmark for Child Education: SOUL Satya Hari School is recognized for its excellence in educating underprivileged children.

Your Donation Matters:

  • Every bit counts:Donate to provide quality education, nutritious meals, and essential supplies for these children.
  • Invest in the future: Your contribution empowers children, strengthens families, and contributes to a brighter Noida.
  • Be part of the change:Join us in creating a world where every child has the opportunity to learn and thrive.
