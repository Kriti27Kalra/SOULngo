About SOUL

Society of Universe Lovers (SOUL) is a NGO registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860. The work under the aegis of SOUL started informally in December 2012 with a school for under-privileged children and a shelter for abandoned cows in Noida. With support, the initiatives grew, which led to the formation of the NGO and it was registered on 19 September 2013.

About US

Helping Each Other can Make World Better

In the year 2021, we adopted Village Pooth on the banks of River Ganga, District Hapur in the State of Uttar Pradesh, India and since then we have been giving free quality education to the tribal kids here. We also give them a mid-day meal daily and work for the empowerment of the illiterate mothers of these children.

SOUL was founded by socially conscious people. It has grown as a movement over the years and many renowned and philanthropic souls support this movement of educating the poor tribal kids.
